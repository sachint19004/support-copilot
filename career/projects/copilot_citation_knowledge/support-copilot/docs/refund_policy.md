# Corporate Refund Policy
## Eligibility Window
Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of their original purchase date. Requests made between 15 and 30 days are eligible only for store credit. No refunds or credits are issued after 30 days.

## Non-Refundable Items
Custom enterprise software deployments, API integration consulting fees, and data migration services are strictly non-refundable once delivery or service kickoff has commenced.
